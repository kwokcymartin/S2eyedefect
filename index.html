How to use
1. Start with normal sight.
2. Drag the near object horizontally.
3. Press Distant object to switch to parallel rays.
4. Change to short-sightedness or long-sightedness.
5. Add a convex or concave lens in front of the eye and compare the focus position.
The external lens position is fixed by the system and cannot be dragged.
